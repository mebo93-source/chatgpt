No. We augment your existing marketing, BD, and proposal teams with specialized strategy, tooling, and execution support.

Most partners see measurable velocity within 60-90 days. We operate in agile sprints with weekly dashboards to track pursuit momentum.

Yes. While most partners focus on North American infrastructure, we collaborate on pursuits in LATAM, EMEA, and APAC as long as we can align on time zones.

Engagements start at $12K/month for six-month retainers and scale with the level of program build-out, staffing, and pursuit volume required.

Do you offer workshops or training?

Absolutely. We facilitate capture planning workshops, pursuit enablement bootcamps, and revenue operations playbook training for leadership teams.
